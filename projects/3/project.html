Smart Hydroponic Farming

The "Smart Hydroponic System" is an IoT-based project designed to monitor and manage hydroponic farming conditions. It features sensors for pH, temperature, and TDS (Total Dissolved Solids), providing real-time data to optimize plant growth. The system is integrated with a website for remote monitoring, allowing users to track and adjust environmental parameters to ensure optimal conditions for their hydroponic setup.
